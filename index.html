Coffees

Flat White

Originating from Australia and New Zealand, the flat white offers a stronger coffee kick with a velvety microfoam texture. It is crafted with a double shot of espresso and steamed milk, giving a rich, full-bodied taste that is smooth yet intense.

Latte

A silky, comforting blend of espresso and steamed milk topped with a light layer of foam. The latte’s mellow flavor makes it ideal for those who enjoy a gentle coffee experience — smooth, creamy, and customizable with flavors like vanilla or caramel.

Cappuccino

A timeless Italian classic made with equal parts espresso, steamed milk, and velvety foam. Its rich aroma and creamy texture create the perfect balance between bold coffee and smooth milk, often finished with a sprinkle of cocoa or cinnamon for warmth and elegance.
